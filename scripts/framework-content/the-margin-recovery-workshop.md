__💰 The Margin Recovery Workshop__

__Reclaim 10–20% Profit Without Increasing Ad Spend__

__🚀 Overview__

Most brands assume more ad spend equals more growth\.  
But in reality, sustainable profit starts with recovery — not expansion\.

The Margin Recovery Workshop walks you through a structured process to identify, prioritize, and fix the biggest profit leaks inside your business\.  
It’s not about cutting costs blindly\.  
It’s about rebuilding financial efficiency through smarter operations, creative optimization, and contribution clarity\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Focuses on immediate profit recovery before scaling\.  
✅ Combines financial clarity with marketing performance\.  
✅ Reveals which areas have the highest ROI potential for repair\.  
✅ Creates structure and accountability for long\-term profit retention\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Audit Every Profit Layer__

You’ll go through five layers of profitability:  
• Product margin \(COGS \+ logistics\)  
• Ad efficiency \(MER, CAC, wasted spend\)  
• Conversion process \(CRO, funnel friction\)  
• Retention \(LTV and repeat order rates\)  
• Team productivity \(cost vs\. output\)

Outcome: Clear visibility on where your biggest inefficiencies lie\.

__2️⃣ Prioritize the Top 3 Fixes__

Each inefficiency is scored by two factors:  
• Profit Impact \(how much it affects your bottom line\)  
• Implementation Time \(how fast it can be fixed\)

Outcome: A shortlist of three high\-ROI actions to execute within 7 days\.

__3️⃣ Build Your Recovery Roadmap__

You’ll get a template that helps you define:  
• Problem statement  
• Fix strategy  
• Expected financial impact  
• Assigned owner and timeline

Outcome: Accountability and measurable recovery progress\.

__4️⃣ Execute, Measure, and Repeat__

Once your roadmap is active, you’ll track improvements weekly\.  
Even small recoveries compound quickly once you remove inefficiencies\.

Outcome: Sustainable profit growth without needing more traffic or spend\.

__📈 Results You Can Expect__

✅ 10–20% increase in monthly net profit within 30 days\.  
✅ Stronger cash flow and liquidity stability\.  
✅ Lower stress through measurable, controlled recovery\.  
✅ A scalable foundation ready for profitable growth\.

__🏁 Final Note__

Scaling only works when your foundation is clean\.

The Margin Recovery Workshop helps you stop the leaks, rebuild efficiency, and create the clarity needed for your next growth phase\.
