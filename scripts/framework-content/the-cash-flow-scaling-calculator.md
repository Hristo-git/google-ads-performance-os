# The Cash Flow Scaling Calculator

Source: The_Cash_Flow_Scaling_Calculator.docx

__How to Know Exactly How Much You Can Spend Without Risking Liquidity__

__🚀 Overview__

Most brands scale until the money runs out\.  
Then they stop, panic, and rebuild from zero\.

That happens because they scale from emotion, not from numbers\.  
They see a good ROAS and increase budget without checking the cash flow impact\.

This calculator gives you a clear structure to scale safely\.  
You will know how much you can reinvest, how fast your money returns, and how to protect your liquidity while growing your revenue\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Protects your cash position while scaling\.  
✅ Reveals your true payback period in days\.  
✅ Calculates your allowable CAC based on margin and goals\.  
✅ Builds confidence to scale with precision, not guesses\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Start With CM2, Not ROAS__

ROAS can look great while profit disappears\.  
CM2 \(Revenue minus COGS minus Marketing\) shows the real picture\.

If CM2 is negative, scaling is dangerous\.  
Only increase ad spend when CM2 is positive for at least two weeks\.

__2️⃣ Calculate Your Allowable CAC__

Use this simple formula to define your safety limit\.

__Allowable CAC = CM1 × Target Profit Margin__

Example  
If your CM1 is 50 euros and you want a 20 percent profit margin, your allowable CAC is 40 euros\.  
Never bid above this number\.

__3️⃣ Anchor Everything to Payback Period__

The payback period shows how fast your investment returns\.

__Formula__  
Payback Period = CAC ÷ Gross Profit per Order

Set your target at 90 days or less\.  
If your payback exceeds that, pause scaling and fix either your margin or your acquisition cost\.

__4️⃣ The 50 30 20 Rule for Safe Reinvestment__

Use profit as your fuel for scaling\.  
Reinvest only a part of it to protect stability\.

Recommended allocation  
• 50 percent → new acquisition campaigns  
• 30 percent → retention and lifecycle marketing  
• 20 percent → profit reserve for future scaling

This ensures that every growth step is cash flow safe\.

__5️⃣ Integrate the Calculator Into Your Routine__

Use a simple Google Sheet to input your weekly numbers\.

Track  
• Revenue  
• Total Marketing Spend  
• CAC  
• CM1 and CM2  
• Payback Period  
• Reinvestment Percentage

The sheet automatically updates your safe scaling limits\.

__6️⃣ Review Weekly, Scale Monthly__

Cash flow is dynamic\.  
Review metrics weekly to stay aligned with your liquidity, but make scaling decisions only once per month\.

This creates enough data stability to make confident moves while avoiding overreactions to short term fluctuations\.

__📈 Results You Can Expect__

✅ Zero liquidity stress while scaling\.  
✅ Clear visibility on how much you can safely reinvest\.  
✅ Faster and safer reinvestment cycles\.  
✅ Predictable, profit\-first growth\.

__🏁 Final Note__

Scaling without cash flow clarity is gambling\.  
Scaling with a clear reinvestment model is controlled growth\.

When your spend decisions are grounded in cash flow logic, you protect your business, your team, and your future growth potential\.
