# The Profitability KPI Glossary

__💹 The Profitability KPI Glossary__

__The 25 Metrics Every D2C Founder Must Know to Scale Safely__

__🚀 Overview__

Most brands scale based on *feeling*, not *financial truth*\.  
They track ROAS, but forget about contribution margin\.  
They know ad spend, but not EBITA\.

This glossary was built to fix that\.

It contains the 25 most important metrics that every brand must understand to protect profitability, track real growth, and scale without risk\.

Each KPI includes a clear definition, formula, benchmark, and practical use case — all written in plain language so your team finally speaks the same “profit language\.”

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Aligns marketing and finance around one shared metric system\.  
✅ Prevents scaling into unprofitable territory\.  
✅ Builds full visibility across acquisition, retention, and operations\.  
✅ Turns decisions from emotional to mathematical\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Acquisition KPIs__

__Cost per Click \(CPC\):__ Average amount paid for each click\.  
__Formula:__ Total Spend ÷ Total Clicks  
__Use:__ Evaluates traffic cost efficiency per channel\.

__Click\-Through Rate \(CTR\):__ Percentage of users who clicked on your ad\.  
__Formula:__ Clicks ÷ Impressions × 100  
__Use:__ Measures ad creative quality and message\-market fit\.

__Cost per Acquisition \(CPA\):__ Cost of acquiring one customer\.  
__Formula:__ Total Spend ÷ Conversions  
__Use:__ Key input for CAC and profit forecasting\.

__Marketing Efficiency Ratio \(MER\):__ The ultimate scaling KPI\.  
__Formula:__ Total Revenue ÷ Total Ad Spend  
__Use:__ Reveals profitability across all channels combined\.

__Return on Ad Spend \(ROAS\):__  
__Formula:__ Revenue ÷ Ad Spend  
__Use:__ Channel\-level efficiency — useful but incomplete without margins\.

__2️⃣ Profitability KPIs__

__Gross Profit Margin \(CM1\):__  
__Formula:__ \(Revenue – COGS\) ÷ Revenue × 100  
__Use:__ Measures product\-level profit before marketing costs\.

__Contribution Margin 2 \(CM2\):__  
__Formula:__ \(Revenue – COGS – Marketing Costs\) ÷ Revenue × 100  
__Use:__ True indicator of profitability after ad spend\.

__EBITA:__  
__Formula:__ Earnings before interest, taxes, and amortization\.  
__Use:__ The final measure of operational profitability\.

__Break\-Even ROAS:__  
__Formula:__ 1 ÷ Gross Margin  
__Use:__ Minimum ROAS needed to avoid loss\.

__Net Profit Margin:__  
__Formula:__ Net Profit ÷ Revenue × 100  
__Use:__ Percentage of total revenue that remains as profit\.

__3️⃣ Retention KPIs__

__Repeat Purchase Rate \(RPR\):__  
__Formula:__ Returning Customers ÷ Total Customers  
__Use:__ Indicates loyalty and LTV health\.

__Customer Lifetime Value \(LTV\):__  
__Formula:__ Average Order Value × Purchase Frequency × Retention Rate  
__Use:__ Measures long\-term customer value beyond first purchase\.

__Customer Acquisition Cost \(CAC\):__  
__Formula:__ Marketing Spend ÷ New Customers Acquired  
__Use:__ When compared to LTV, reveals scalability\.

__LTV:CAC Ratio:__  
__Formula:__ LTV ÷ CAC  
__Use:__ The core profitability metric\. Aim for 3:1 or higher\.

__Churn Rate:__  
__Formula:__ Lost Customers ÷ Total Customers  
__Use:__ Tracks how fast your customer base erodes\.

__4️⃣ Operational KPIs__

__Average Order Value \(AOV\):__  
__Formula:__ Total Revenue ÷ Number of Orders  
__Use:__ Key driver for increasing profit per transaction\.

__Refund Rate:__  
__Formula:__ Refunded Orders ÷ Total Orders  
__Use:__ Impacts both cash flow and margin accuracy\.

__Fulfillment Cost per Order:__  
__Formula:__ Total Fulfillment Cost ÷ Orders  
__Use:__ Determines operational scalability\.

__Inventory Turnover:__  
__Formula:__ COGS ÷ Average Inventory  
__Use:__ Measures how efficiently stock is sold and replaced\.

__Cash Conversion Cycle \(CCC\):__  
__Formula:__ Days Inventory \+ Days Receivable – Days Payable  
__Use:__ Tracks how quickly capital returns to the business\.

__5️⃣ Scaling KPIs__

__Blended MER Trend:__  
__Formula:__ Revenue ÷ Total Ad Spend \(over time\)  
__Use:__ Monitors efficiency across platforms during growth\.

__Incremental ROAS \(iROAS\):__  
__Formula:__ Revenue Increase ÷ Spend Increase  
__Use:__ Determines the real impact of scaling\.

__Marketing Reinvestment Ratio:__  
__Formula:__ Ad Spend ÷ Profit  
__Use:__ Measures how aggressively you reinvest into growth\.

__Return on Capital Employed \(ROCE\):__  
__Formula:__ EBITA ÷ \(Total Assets – Current Liabilities\)  
__Use:__ Evaluates how efficiently capital drives profit\.

__Scaling Safety Index:__  
__Formula:__ EBITA Stability \+ Cash Flow Coverage \+ CM2 Consistency  
__Use:__ Internal benchmark to ensure you scale with margin protection\.
