## Key Formulas Quick Reference

**MER** = Total Revenue ÷ Total Ad Spend (minimum 4 for safe scaling)
**CM2** = (Revenue - COGS - Marketing) ÷ Revenue × 100
**Cost Efficiency** = (Revenue - Ad Spend) ÷ Ad Spend
**LTV:CAC Ratio** = LTV ÷ CAC (aim for 3:1 or higher)
**Break-Even ROAS** = 1 ÷ Gross Margin

All formulas with full context are in The_Profitability_KPI_Glossary.docx
