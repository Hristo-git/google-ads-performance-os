__🧠 The Attribution Clarity Blueprint__

__How to Decode Your True ROI Across Google, Meta and Email__

__🚀 Overview__

Most brands do not have an attribution problem\.  
They have an interpretation problem\.

When Google says one thing, Meta says another, and Klaviyo takes credit for everything, chaos wins and profit disappears\.

The result?  
You scale the wrong channels, pause the right ones, and lose clarity on what truly drives growth\.

This blueprint shows you how to cut through the noise, unify your data, and finally understand where your profit comes from\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Clear Financial Attribution → Connect revenue, spend and margins across all channels\.  
✅ Data Driven Scaling Decisions → Know what to scale, pause or fix with confidence\.  
✅ Cross Channel Profit View → See the real impact of each channel, not inflated platform numbers\.  
✅ Aligned Reporting → One truth for your team, your agency and your CFO\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ MER Over ROAS__

ROAS shows vanity\. MER shows reality\.  
MER \(Marketing Efficiency Ratio = Revenue ÷ Total Marketing Spend\) is your main profit indicator\.

Rule  
• MER ≥ 4 → Scaling is safe  
• MER < 3 → Diagnose margin or CAC leak

MER is the only metric that unites all channels under one system\.

__2️⃣ The Attribution Pyramid__

__Top Layer: Platform Data \(Directional\)__  
Google, Meta, Klaviyo dashboards show patterns, not truth\.

__Middle Layer: Blended Metrics \(Operational\)__  
MER, CAC, Payback show system efficiency and cash flow speed\.

__Bottom Layer: Profit Data \(Source of Truth\)__  
CM2, EBITA, Cash Flow reveal if growth is healthy and sustainable\.

Decision Rule  
Only scale when all three layers align\.

__3️⃣ The 72 Hour Attribution Window__

Do not judge performance too early\.

Conversion delays vary:  
• Meta up to 72 hours post click  
• Google up to 24 hours  
• Klaviyo often influenced but rarely first touch

Wait three days before making scaling decisions to avoid cutting campaigns that convert later\.
