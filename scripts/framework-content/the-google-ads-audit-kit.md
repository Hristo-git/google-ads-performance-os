# The Google Ads Audit Kit

__🔍 The Google Ads Audit Kit__

__Find Hidden Waste and Missed Profit in 30 Minutes__

__🚀 Overview__

Most Google Ads accounts lose profit quietly\.  
Wrong attribution, broken conversions, mismatched bids — small leaks that compound into thousands of wasted dollars every month\.

This audit kit exposes those leaks fast\.

It’s a 30\-minute diagnostic checklist built to help you uncover inefficiencies, spot scaling blockers, and identify where profit is lost inside your Google Ads setup\.

No fluff, no guesswork — just the exact checkpoints that separate profitable accounts from average ones\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Reveals inefficiencies before they destroy profit\.  
✅ Creates a clean foundation for scaling\.  
✅ Works for any account size or campaign type\.  
✅ Saves time with a structured, repeatable audit process\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Account Structure Audit__

Check:  
• Campaign naming consistency and logical grouping\.  
• Segmentation between branded, non\-branded, Shopping, and Performance Max\.  
• Search term overlap between campaigns\.

Goal: Ensure your structure supports clarity, control, and data separation\.

__2️⃣ Conversion Tracking Audit__

Check:  
• All conversion actions are firing correctly\.  
• No double\-counted events between platforms\.  
• Correct attribution model \(prefer data\-driven\)\.  
• Value tracking for actual revenue, not just transactions\.

Goal: Know that every click and sale in your reports reflects the truth\.

__3️⃣ Budget Distribution Audit__

Check:  
• Budget aligns with campaign profitability \(MER or ROAS\)\.  
• No underperforming campaigns consuming excess spend\.  
• Correct bid strategies \(manual vs automated\) per campaign maturity\.

Goal: Shift spend from vanity campaigns to profit\-producing ones\.

__4️⃣ Search & Shopping Optimization Audit__

Check:  
• Negative keyword list coverage\.  
• Search term reports for wasted spend\.  
• Product feed optimization \(titles, images, attributes\)\.  
• Smart bidding alignment with real conversion data\.

Goal: Eliminate non\-converting clicks and improve ad relevance\.

__5️⃣ Creative & Ad Copy Audit__

Check:  
• Ad extensions are used effectively \(sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets\)\.  
• CTR benchmarks per campaign type\.  
• Headline and description alignment with search intent\.  
• Test variants have meaningful differences, not cosmetic ones\.

Goal: Improve engagement and CTR without increasing spend\.

__6️⃣ Geographic & Device Performance Audit__

Check:  
• Top\-performing regions and devices receive higher bid adjustments\.  
• Low\-converting areas or devices are excluded or limited\.  
• Delivery time\-of\-day performance analyzed for bid scheduling\.

Goal: Focus ad delivery where conversion efficiency is highest\.

__7️⃣ Profitability Alignment Audit__

Check:  
• Each campaign’s MER and CM2 are tracked weekly\.  
• High\-volume keywords are cross\-referenced with margin per SKU\.  
• Lifetime value of customers acquired by channel is understood\.

Goal: Connect ad performance with profit, not just revenue\.

__📈 Results You Can Expect__

✅ 10–20% lower wasted spend within 30 days\.  
✅ Higher data accuracy across all ad channels\.  
✅ Clear visibility into scaling opportunities\.  
✅ Stronger profitability from the same ad budget\.
