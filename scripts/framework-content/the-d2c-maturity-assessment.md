__🧭 The D2C Maturity Assessment__

__Discover Exactly How Scalable Your Brand Really Is__

__🚀 Overview__

Most D2C founders believe they’re ready to scale — until the data says otherwise\.

This self\-assessment reveals the truth about your business readiness across five critical dimensions:  
__Data, Profitability, Systems, Retention, and Creative Infrastructure\.__

It’s a scorecard\-style framework used by top\-performing brands to identify bottlenecks, uncover hidden profit leaks, and determine how close \(or far\) you are from scalable growth\.

In 10 minutes, you’ll know whether your brand is in *Foundation*, *Acceleration*, or *Optimization* phase — and what to fix first\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Creates immediate clarity on your brand’s scalability\.  
✅ Identifies weak links before scaling exposes them\.  
✅ Provides a clear, data\-backed action plan for each stage\.  
✅ Turns gut feeling into measurable performance indicators\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Data Accuracy__

Question: Can you fully trust your numbers?

Score yourself 1–5 based on:  
• Conversion tracking setup and accuracy\.  
• Consistent UTM tagging across channels\.  
• Weekly data reviews and automated anomaly alerts\.  
• Connected dashboards for MER, CM2, and LTV\.

A score below 3 means scaling will be risky until fixed\.

__2️⃣ Profitability Control__

Question: Do you know your real profit per order?

Score yourself 1–5 based on:  
• Clarity on contribution margins \(CM1–CM3\)\.  
• Cost structure tracking \(COGS, shipping, fulfillment, marketing\)\.  
• Cash flow forecasting and financial modeling\.  
• Profit\-first decision\-making culture\.

Below 4? Your scaling budget might be growing faster than your margin\.

__3️⃣ Systemization__

Question: Can your operations handle double the volume?

Score yourself 1–5 based on:  
• Documented SOPs for marketing, fulfillment, and support\.  
• Automated workflows and campaign triggers\.  
• Clear ownership for recurring tasks\.  
• Scalability of your tech stack and logistics partners\.

Below 3? Your systems will break before your ads do\.

__4️⃣ Retention & LTV__

Question: Are you maximizing value from existing customers?

Score yourself 1–5 based on:  
• Post\-purchase flows and reactivation campaigns\.  
• Subscription or loyalty programs\.  
• Customer segmentation and re\-engagement timing\.  
• LTV measurement and retention dashboards\.

Below 3? You’re overpaying for growth and under\-monetizing your base\.

__5️⃣ Creative & Testing Infrastructure__

Question: How fast can you test new ad angles or creatives?

Score yourself 1–5 based on:  
• Number of new creatives launched weekly\.  
• Testing framework for hooks and formats\.  
• Connection between ad data and creative decisions\.  
• Collaboration between creative and performance teams\.

Below 4? Your growth is capped by creative fatigue\.

__📈 Results You Can Expect__

✅ Full clarity on your current maturity level\.  
✅ Immediate identification of the next bottleneck to solve\.  
✅ Benchmarks to track progress toward scalable operations\.  
✅ A data\-driven roadmap for your next growth stage\.

It’s about evolving faster\.

When you measure where your brand truly stands, you can stop guessing — and start building a business that scales with confidence\.

The D2C Maturity Assessment gives you that clarity\.
