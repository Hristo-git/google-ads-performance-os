__📘 BFCM Google Ads Playbook__

__How to Dominate Black Friday and Cyber Monday with Profit\-First Google Ads__

__🚀 Overview__

Last year, our BFCM frameworks generated over $100K in sales at a 10x ROAS for one partner brand\.  
This year, we’ve upgraded the system to help your brand scale profitably through the most competitive quarter of the year\.

If you want to turn Q4 into your highest\-profit season yet, start here\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Capture demand before it peaks  
✅ Build trust early to convert cheaper  
✅ Segment audiences for precision targeting  
✅ Align email, ads, and offers for higher profit  
✅ Scale with data, not discounts

__1️⃣ Capture Leads Early__

Before November traffic spikes, use Google Ads to collect high\-intent leads while CPCs are still low\.

__Setup:__

- 60% Search \+ 40% YouTube or Demand Gen
- Focus on educational and curiosity\-based searches like *“why am I always tired”* or *“how to stay focused during workouts”*
- Build awareness pages that teach before selling

__Pro Tip:__  
Run pre\-sale traffic to *“Black Friday supplement deals”* style pages and capture early\-access signups\.

__2️⃣ Segment for Precision__

Once you collect leads, divide them into four profit segments:

__Segment__

__Goal__

__Message__

__New subscribers__

Build trust

Use first\-time offers, urgency, and proof

__Existing customers__

Retain

Reward loyalty and offer early access

__High\-value customers__

Maximize AOV

Push premium bundles and exclusives

__Lapsed customers__

Reactivate

Offer win\-back codes or free shipping

Tailor ad copy, creatives, and landing pages to each segment for maximum conversion\.

__3️⃣ Convert Like a Pro__

Your conversion engine needs personalized campaigns per audience\.

__New subscribers:__ urgency \+ social proof  
__Existing customers:__ exclusivity \+ reward  
__High\-value:__ premium messaging  
__Lapsed:__ reactivation with value incentive

Align your __email flows__ with these segments and amplify with retargeting\.

__4️⃣ Optimize Your Landing Pages__

Your landing page is your profit gate\.

__Checklist:__

- Fast load time
- Mobile optimized
- Trust elements above the fold
- Clear CTAs and urgency blocks

Create one high\-intent page per segment for best performance\.

__5️⃣ Leverage Email & SMS__

Most BFCM revenue comes from your __warm audience__, not new traffic\.

Build automated flows that sell while you sleep:

- Early\-access announcements
- VIP drops for loyal buyers
- Cart recovery \+ countdowns
- Win\-back messages for inactive lists

__6️⃣ The Real BFCM Secret__

Winning BFCM isn’t about discounting harder\.  
It’s about __warming up your audience before the sale begins\.__

Brands that pre\-build intent see up to 40–60% more revenue during BFCM week — without higher ad spend\.

__🏁 Final Note__

The biggest BFCM wins come from brands that start early, segment smart, and build profit systems before the chaos hits\.
