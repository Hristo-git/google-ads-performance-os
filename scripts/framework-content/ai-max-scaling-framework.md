__⚡ AI Max Scaling Framework__

__How to Use Google’s New Search System to Scale Profitably in 2025__

__🚀 Overview__

Google has introduced __AI Max__, a new Search campaign type that uses artificial intelligence to understand __user intent beyond keywords__\.

Most brands have not yet learned how to control it, which means early adopters can gain a real advantage\.

We have tested AI Max across multiple ecommerce brands and built a system that __scales revenue without increasing CAC__ or __hurting PMax performance__\.

This playbook shows how to set it up correctly and use it for profit\-first growth\.

__🎯 Why It Matters__

✅ AI Max targets intent instead of single keywords  
✅ It creates ad variations that fit each user’s search behavior  
✅ It discovers profitable queries that regular Search misses  
✅ It improves over time when managed correctly

Without structure, it wastes spend\.  
With the right process, it becomes a reliable growth lever\.

__🧩 What Makes AI Max Different__

Two key mechanisms make AI Max powerful

__Behavioral Query Matching__  
It studies user behavior to identify search intent instead of relying on exact matches\.

__Dynamic Asset Customization__  
It automatically builds headlines and text from your site and adapts them to each search\.

This creates personalized relevance at scale when guided by clear rules\.

__⚙️ Step 1: Smart Setup__

Use these settings for the first build

__Turn On__  
• Search Term Matching  
• Text Customization

__Use Carefully__  
• Final URL Expansion

If you have multiple strong product or category pages, Final URL Expansion can match users to the best destination\.  
If you sell one core product or have limited content, turn it off and set URLs manually to keep full control\.

__🧭 Step 2: Control the Traffic__

AI Max learns quickly, so structure matters

__Brand Exclusions__  
Remove your brand keywords so AI Max does not steal from your branded campaigns\.

__Brand Inclusions__  
Only include your brand for competitor\-focused campaigns\. Keep it off for prospecting\.

__URL Rules__  
• Use inclusions at ad group level to send traffic to specific product pages\.  
• Use exclusions at campaign level to block outdated or low\-performing pages\.

__Location Settings__  
Choose *Presence only* so your ads reach users physically located in your target markets\.

__🧠 Step 3: Expect a Learning Curve__

During the first one to two weeks, results may look unstable\.  
That is normal\.

AI Max explores a wide audience before it learns which segments perform best\.  
Once stabilized, it usually beats standard Search in reach and relevance\.  
Be patient before judging success\.

__🧾 Step 4: Weekly Optimization Routine__

Review the following every five to seven days

✅ __Search Terms__ Remove irrelevant queries and promote top converters into Exact Match campaigns\.  
✅ __Auto\-Generated Assets__ Check that the text and headlines are accurate and on brand\.  
✅ __Performance Comparison__ Compare with Search and PMax\. Ensure AI Max is not stealing budget or branded traffic\.

Consistent review keeps the system efficient and protects your ROI\.

__📈 Step 5: Scale the Winners__

Once performance is stable, expand carefully

• Increase budgets on top\-performing asset groups by about fifteen percent\.  
• Add new ad groups by product type or key benefit\.  
• Separate campaigns by SKU profitability\.

__High\-margin products__ Use Target ROAS\.  
__Low\-margin products__ Use Maximize Conversions or Target CPA\.

Monitor contribution margins and payback period to stay profitable as you scale\.

__💡 Key Insight__

AI Max is not an autopilot tool\.  
It works best as an assistant with clear guidance\.

Most brands will rely entirely on automation and lose control\.  
You can win by structuring it properly, monitoring weekly, and training it through clear signals\.

When done well, AI Max quietly outperforms standard Search and opens new profitable reach\.
