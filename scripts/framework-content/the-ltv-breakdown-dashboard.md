# The LTV Breakdown Dashboard

Source: The_LTV_Breakdown_Dashboard.docx

__📊 The LTV Breakdown Dashboard__

__See Exactly Which Products Create Your Most Profitable Customers__

__🚀 Overview__

Most brands know their total LTV but not *where it comes from*\.  
They treat every product and channel equally — and end up scaling the wrong ones\.

This dashboard changes that\.

The LTV Breakdown Dashboard gives you a plug\-and\-play spreadsheet to calculate customer lifetime value by __SKU, category, and acquisition source__ — revealing which products actually create long\-term profit and which ones quietly destroy it\.

It’s your financial x\-ray for smarter scaling decisions\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Connects acquisition data with true profitability over time\.  
✅ Highlights which SKUs attract repeat customers\.  
✅ Shows which channels drive the highest\-value buyers\.  
✅ Transforms LTV from a vanity metric into a strategic decision tool\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Collect the Right Data__

Goal: Merge transactional and marketing data into one view\.

Checklist:  
• Export customer order data from Shopify \(email, SKU, order value, date\)\.  
• Export acquisition source data from Google Ads and Meta \(UTMs or first\-click\)\.  
• Combine both datasets by customer ID or email\.

Outcome: Unified data showing which channel brought which customer — and what they bought next\.

__2️⃣ Calculate Product\-Level LTV__

Goal: Measure how each SKU performs across time\.

Formula:  
__LTV per SKU = \(Total Revenue from Customers Who First Bought SKU X\) ÷ \(Number of Those Customers\)__

Add time filters \(30, 60, 90, 180, 365 days\) to see how quickly each SKU compounds value\.

Outcome: Clear visibility into which products attract long\-term customers\.

__3️⃣ Calculate Channel\-Level LTV__

Goal: Identify which traffic source brings the best long\-term buyers\.

Formula:  
__LTV per Channel = \(Total Revenue from Customers by Channel\) ÷ \(Customers from That Channel\)__

Compare results from Google, Meta, Email, and other sources\.  
Pair with CAC to calculate __LTV:CAC ratio__ per channel\.

Outcome: Data\-driven clarity on where to scale spend safely\.

__4️⃣ Find “Gateway” Products__

Goal: Discover which products create your most loyal buyers\.

Checklist:  
• Identify SKUs that lead to multiple repeat orders\.  
• Note their first\-purchase price point and margin\.  
• Look for correlation between entry product and LTV after 180 days\.

Outcome: Pinpoint which products should receive priority in ads, bundles, and retention flows\.

__5️⃣ Monitor and Update Monthly__

Goal: Track trends and compounding effects\.

Checklist:  
• Update dashboard monthly with new data\.  
• Add filters for cohorts by acquisition date\.  
• Track LTV growth over time for retained customers\.  
• Use insights to reallocate ad spend or product focus\.

Outcome: A living system that continuously improves scaling efficiency\.

__📈 Results You Can Expect__

✅ Clear insight into which products and channels drive long\-term profit\.  
✅ Smarter ad spend allocation toward high\-LTV SKUs\.  
✅ Improved retention and product strategy alignment\.  
✅ Predictable profitability through data clarity\.

__🏁 Final Note__

LTV is not a single number — it’s a map of where your best customers come from\.

When you know exactly which products and channels create high\-value buyers, every marketing dollar becomes a long\-term investment, not a short\-term expense\.

The LTV Breakdown Dashboard gives you the clarity to scale profitably — and permanently\.
