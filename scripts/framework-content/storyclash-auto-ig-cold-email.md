__Influencer Marketing Guide: Storyclash \+ AutoIGDM \+ Cold Email__

__Part A – Finding the Right Creators with Storyclash__

__1\) Define Your Fundamentals__

- __Goal__: sales, awareness, or content assets\.
- __Platform__: Instagram\.
- __Creator Type__: Nano, Micro, Mid\.
- __Region, language, niche__\.
- __Collaboration model__: paid collab, affiliate/code, gifting, or UGC\.

__2\) Creator Discovery__

- Use __Influencer Discovery__ inside Storyclash\.
- Search by __keywords, hashtags, content style, or performance filters__\.
- __Competitor check__: see which creators work with similar brands\.
- Build a __shortlist__ directly in Storyclash\.

__3\) Evaluate & Select__

- __Content fit__: does the style align with your brand?
- __Performance__: strong engagement and steady growth\.
- __Region/language__: aligned with your target audience\.
- __Past collaborations__: any relevant brand partnerships?

__4\) Export__

- Export the shortlist as __CSV/Excel__\.
- Use this file for __DM and email outreach__\.

__Part B – Instagram DM Outreach with AutoIGDM__

__5\) Setup & Safety__

- Create an AutoIGDM account and connect your IG profile\.
- __Warm\-up__: 2–3 days of light “human\-like” activity\.
- Start with ~20 DMs/day and slowly scale up to a max of 50/day\.

__6\) DM Templates \(short & casual\)__

__Basic Collab Check__

Hey \{firstname\}, do you also do brand collabs? Would you be interested?

__Quick Collab Hook__

Hey \{firstname\}, would you be down for a quick collab with our brand?

__Follow\-up 1__

Just checking in \{firstname\} – should I send you the details?

__Follow\-up 2__

Hey \{firstname\}, no rush – but want to see what we had in mind?

__Part C – Cold Email Outreach with Storyclash__

__7\) Cold Email Fundamentals__

__1\. Domain Setup__

- Never use your main shop/company domain\.
- Buy a separate domain just for outreach \(e\.g\., if your brand is *brand\.com*, use *brand\-collabs\.com*\)\.
- This protects your main domain from spam filters or blacklisting\.

__2\. Email Warm\-up__

- Warm up new domains/accounts for at least __2–3 weeks__\.
- Send and reply to a few real emails daily\.
- Gradually increase sending volume before starting cold outreach\.

__3\. Sending Limits__

- Per email account: __max\. 50–100 emails/day__\.
- Use multiple inboxes if you need higher volume\.

__4\. Core Cold Email Rules__

- Keep emails __super short__ \(3–5 sentences\)\.
- Use __very simple subject lines__ \(looks like a normal email, not spam\)\.
- Avoid attachments, images, too many links\.
- Always end with a __clear, simple question__\.

__8\) Cold Email Templates__

__Subject Line Examples \(2–3 words max\):__

- “Quick question”
- “Collab?”
- “Collaboration”
- “Idea for you”

__Email Example 1 – Basic Collab Check__

Hey \{firstname\},  

I checked out your content – looks like a great fit for our brand\.  

Do you also do collaborations?  

If yes, I’d be happy to send you the details\.  

Best,  

\[Your Name\]  

__Email Example 2 – Quick Collab Hook__

Hey \{firstname\},  

Would you be interested in doing a quick collab with us?  

I can send over the details if you’d like\.  

Cheers,  

\[Your Name\]  

__Email Example 3 – Follow\-up 1 \(after 3–4 days\)__

Hi \{firstname\},  

Just following up – should I send you the collab details?  

Best,  

\[Your Name\]  

__Email Example 4 – Follow\-up 2 \(after 5–7 days\)__

Hey \{firstname\},  

No rush – but would you like to see what we had in mind?  

Best,  

\[Your Name\]  

__Part D – Measuring Success__

__9\) Tracking__

- For email: use __unique tracking links or discount codes__ \(share only after reply, not in first cold email\)\.
- For DMs: track replies and positive responses\.

__10\) Reporting__

- In Storyclash: create reports with key KPIs \(reach, engagement, ROI\)\.
- For email: track reply rates, positive replies, and conversions into collabs\.

__Part E – Compliance & Safety__

- __Instagram DMs__: No links in the first message, max\. 50/day, clean accounts only\.
- __Cold Emails__: Always send from secondary domains, warm them up, respect daily limits\.
- __Tone__: casual, short, human – not pushy\. The goal is a __reply__, not a full pitch\.

__Quick Checklist__

1. Use Storyclash to find and shortlist creators\.
2. Export CSV\.
3. AutoIGDM: warm\-up → upload list → start DM outreach\.
4. Cold Email: setup new domain \+ warm\-up, send 50–100/day\.
5. Use short subject lines\.
6. Keep messages to 3–5 sentences\.
7. Add clear follow\-ups\.
8. Track results in Storyclash\.
