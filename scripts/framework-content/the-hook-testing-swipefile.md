__⚡ The Hook Testing Swipefile__

__Proven Openings That Stop the Scroll and Drive Clicks__

__🚀 Overview__

The first three seconds decide everything\.  
If your hook fails, your ad never gets read, watched, or remembered — no matter how good the product or offer is\.

This swipefile gives you 100 proven hooks categorized by emotional trigger, awareness level, and platform intent\.  
It’s built for data\-driven creative testing, not random inspiration\.

You’ll learn exactly how to structure hooks that pull attention, spark curiosity, and create instant relevance — while staying true to your brand’s tone\.

__🎯 Why This Works__

✅ Removes guesswork from creative testing\.  
✅ Helps teams generate high\-performing ad variations in minutes\.  
✅ Covers every major emotional trigger used in top\-converting ads\.  
✅ Works for video scripts, headlines, email subject lines, and ad copy\.

__🧩 The Framework__

__1️⃣ Curiosity Hooks__

Used to spark intrigue without revealing the full story\.  
Examples:  
• “Nobody talks about this metric, but it predicts 80% of your profit\.”  
• “We tested this for 90 days — the result shocked us\.”  
• “Most brands ignore this one setting in Google Ads\.”

When to use: Cold audiences or top\-of\-funnel content where you need attention fast\.

__2️⃣ Contrarian Hooks__

Used to challenge assumptions and reset beliefs\.  
Examples:  
• “High ROAS is killing your profit\.”  
• “Discounts don’t build loyalty — they destroy it\.”  
• “More traffic isn’t the goal\. More margin is\.”

When to use: Thought leadership content, case studies, or scaling ads targeting experienced operators\.

__3️⃣ Emotional Hooks__

Used to connect through pain or desire\.  
Examples:  
• “You work 12 hours a day but your revenue won’t grow\.”  
• “What if scaling didn’t cost your sanity?”  
• “Finally, a system that protects your margins while you scale\.”

When to use: Retargeting or narrative\-driven ads\.

__4️⃣ Logical Hooks__

Used to appeal to reason and structure\.  
Examples:  
• “Your MER dropped because your LTV isn’t keeping up\.”  
• “This dashboard reveals which country delivers the best EBITA\.”  
• “Stop scaling until these three metrics are green\.”

When to use: Educating mid\-funnel audiences or decision makers\.

__5️⃣ Outcome Hooks__

Used to anchor the promise in a tangible result\.  
Examples:  
• “How we tripled profit with the same ad spend\.”  
• “From 200K to 1\.2M per month in 4 months\.”  
• “How to dominate your niche with just one product\.”

When to use: Performance\-based campaigns or case study creative\.

__6️⃣ Social Proof Hooks__

Used to borrow authority and credibility\.  
Examples:  
• “Over 1,000 brands trust this scaling framework\.”  
• “What we learned after managing $50M in ad spend\.”  
• “This one tweak improved a client’s MER by 37%\.”

When to use: Cold audiences that need proof before curiosity\.

__7️⃣ Urgency Hooks__

Used to drive immediate action\.  
Examples:  
• “Last 48 hours before we close new clients\.”  
• “Only 3 seats left for the next Profit Scaling cohort\.”  
• “This strategy stops working once competitors catch on\.”

When to use: Launches, seasonal offers, or short\-window campaigns\.

__🧠 Implementation Tip__

Each hook should be tested across two formats — *video opening* and *headline*\.  
Keep variables small: only the first line changes, everything else stays consistent\.  
This isolates the performance of the hook itself, giving you clean creative learning data\.
